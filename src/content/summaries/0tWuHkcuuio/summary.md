## Summary

The ChatGPT App Store has officially launched, presenting a new platform for developers with initial categories like Featured, Lifestyle, and Productivity. Early apps relevant to product builders include official integrations from Figma, Aha! for roadmapping, and Lovable, which turns conversations into working applications.

However, its potential is questioned due to its nature as an "app within an app" and data showing most ChatGPT usage occurs on mobile devices. This makes it suitable for **transactional conversational use cases** (e.g., Instacart, Open

Table) but less ideal for complex tasks like building coded apps, where native applications might be superior.

For product strategy, **AI app marketplaces** (from OpenAI and Anthropic, which just updated its Connectors library) and **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** integrations are highlighted as two major new distribution channels for 2025. The video suggests a future shift where users may interact with products through these AI interfaces rather than opening the core app directly.

### Other Major AI Developments

*   **Google** launched an experimental AI agent for Gmail called **CC** (in beta) that summarizes your day using calendar, drive, and web data. They also released **OPEL** in Gemini, a tool for building AI mini-apps with natural language.

*   **11 Labs** launched its **conversational voice and chat agents** on Whats

App, enabling multi-platform deployment for customer service.

*   **Door

Dash** released **Zesty**, a Gen Z-focused app for restaurant discovery via hyper-specific conversational searches (e.g., "a lowkey dinner for introverts"). This represents a strategic move into the **pre-transaction discovery layer** to gather rich intent data.

### Tools, Data & Trends

*   **Tools**: `Manipi` (turns websites into APIs), `Craft` (Mac App of the Year for notes/tasks), and `Monles` (a focus app that blurs background windows).

*   **Productivity Data**: A 2025 State of AI Coding report shows **medium-sized engineering teams (6-15 devs) more than doubled their code output** (from ~7k to over 13k lines), highlighting the multiplier effect of AI-augmented work.

*   **Market Analysis**: Similar

Web data shows OpenAI's **Sora app** (for AI-generated video) peaked at 1M daily users, suggesting niche stickiness. **Notebook LM** is a success for Google with ~130M visitors. The report also notes a significant crossover, with **14.2% of Google visitors also using ChatGPT** in 2025, up from 1.6% in 2023.
